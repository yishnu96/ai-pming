# Neobrutalism Design Principles

Neobrutalism (or neubrutalism) is an evolution of traditional brutalism. It is a visual-design trend defined by high contrast, blocky layouts, bold colors, thick borders, and "unpolished" elements.

## Core Characteristics

1. **High Contrast and Bright Colors**
   - Uses bold, primary colors and high-contrast combinations.
   - Emphasizes key functions and UI elements.
   - Creates an unconventional, memorable experience.

2. **Thick Lines and Geometric Shapes**
   - Uses thick borders, angular forms, and solid lines.
   - Creates structure without relying on gradients or soft shadows.

3. **Stark Drop Shadows**
   - Encourages bold, striking shadows instead of soft, layered ones.
   - Incorporates solid, single-color shadows (e.g., a black drop shadow offset by 4px or 8px with 0px blur).
   - Adds depth while maintaining a "raw" aesthetic.

4. **Bold Type**
   - Promotes the use of bold, "unpolished" elements.
   - Often includes quirky or slightly eccentric typefaces.
   - Balanced by a generous use of whitespace.

5. **Skeuomorphic Elements**
   - Incorporates nostalgic elements from early digital interfaces (e.g., Windows 98-style buttons, monospace fonts).
   - Blends retro aesthetics with modern design.

## Best Practices for Implementation

While neobrutalism thrives on bold colors and sharp contrasts, it can overwhelm users if not balanced correctly.

- **Design with Usability at the Forefront**: Prioritize usability with clear buttons, readable type, and ample whitespace.
- **Contrast Ratios Matter**: Bold colors must meet text-contrast standards. Avoid pairing vibrant hues that fail readability tests.
- **Limit Your Color Palette**: Restrict your palette to 2-3 bold, high-contrast colors (e.g., black, neon green, electric blue).
- **Prioritize Readability**: Pair bold, unconventional headlines with clean, neutral body fonts (like Roboto or Inter). Avoid overly decorative typefaces for paragraphs.
- **Use Whitespace Strategically**: Offset dense geometric shapes and thick borders with generous padding (e.g., 24-32px margins) to create breathing room.
- **Test Interactions**: Ensure interactive elements remain recognizable. Use underlines on hover or subtle color shifts/translations to indicate state changes.
- **Avoid Oversimplification**: Retain hierarchy through size variation (e.g., headlines twice as large as body text) and color intensity.
