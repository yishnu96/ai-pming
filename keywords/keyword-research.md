## Opportunity Scoring Formula

```
Opportunity Score = (Volume x (1 - Difficulty/100)) x Intent Multiplier
```

**Intent Multipliers:**
- Transactional: 1.5x
- Commercial Investigation: 1.3x
- Informational: 1.0x
- Navigational: 0.2x

## Start Here — Top 5 Keywords

These are the 5 keywords to target first, chosen for: low difficulty, clear intent, high opportunity score, and ability to anchor or support a cluster.

### 1. "how to use chatgpt for beginners"
- **Why now:** Highest volume (12,000/mo) with manageable difficulty (38). Every beginner searches this. Anchors Cluster 2.
- **Page type:** Comprehensive step-by-step tutorial with screenshots
- **Cluster role:** Pillar page for ChatGPT & Prompt Skills cluster
- **Target SERP features:** Featured snippet, People Also Ask

### 2. "what is generative ai for beginners"
- **Why now:** Hot topic (5,200/mo), moderate difficulty (32). Generative AI is what most people mean when they say "AI" today. Anchors Cluster 1.
- **Page type:** Educational explainer guide with examples and visuals
- **Cluster role:** Key supporting page for AI Fundamentals cluster (can serve as alternate pillar)
- **Target SERP features:** Featured snippet, AI Overview

### 3. "ai tools for writing emails"
- **Why now:** Low difficulty (25), commercial intent (1.3x multiplier), practical value. Everyone writes emails — this bridges AI literacy with real daily use.
- **Page type:** Tool roundup with comparison table and examples
- **Cluster role:** Supporting page for AI Tools for Productivity cluster
- **Target SERP features:** People Also Ask, Featured Snippet

### 4. "ai for product managers"
- **Why now:** Low difficulty (22), commercial intent, highly targeted audience. PM community is active and shares content — strong for community building.
- **Page type:** Role-specific guide with use cases, tools, and workflows
- **Cluster role:** Supporting page for AI for Your Career cluster
- **Target SERP features:** People Also Ask, Featured Snippet

### 5. "how to use ai for daily tasks"
- **Why now:** Very low difficulty (15), perfect beginner keyword. Bridges all three segments — literacy, tools, and profession. Great internal linking hub.
- **Page type:** Practical listicle guide (10-15 daily tasks with AI walkthroughs)
- **Cluster role:** Supporting page for AI Tools for Productivity cluster
- **Target SERP features:** Featured Snippet, People Also Ask
