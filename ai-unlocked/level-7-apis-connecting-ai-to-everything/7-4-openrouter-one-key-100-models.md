## What Is OpenRouter?

<Star1 color="#fbbf24" stroke="black" strokeWidth={2} size={36} style={{flexShrink: 0}} />

</div>

**OpenRouter** — a unified API platform that connects to 100+ AI models from different companies through one single endpoint. For example, instead of getting separate API keys from OpenAI, Google, Meta, and Mistral, you get one OpenRouter key and use it to access all of them.

### The Streaming Service Analogy

Think about this scenario without AI:

- You need one AI for writing emails (say, Claude)
- A different AI for summarizing documents (Gemini)
- Another for creative brainstorming (Mistral)

Without OpenRouter, each means: a separate sign-up, a separate API key, a separate billing dashboard, and a separate balance to track. That's four different logins just to do your work.

With OpenRouter: one account, one key, you pick whichever model fits the task.

### What It's NOT

OpenRouter is not an AI model itself. It does not create its own intelligence. Think of it as the bridge between you and the models — not the destination. OpenRouter handles the boring stuff so you can focus on using the right model for the job: automatic provider fallbacks if one goes down, analytics to track your usage in real time, and quota controls so you never overspend.

> **Think About It:** If you needed three different AI models for your daily work, which would you prefer — managing three separate accounts or one OpenRouter hub? Write down the answer.

## Free Models That Actually Work

Here's where OpenRouter gets interesting: it offers **28 free models** you can use with zero cost and no credit card required.

### Real Free, Not "Free Trial"

This distinction matters. A "free trial" means: try a few times, then pay up. OpenRouter's free models are genuinely free. They won't expire after seven days. You don't need to enter card details. They stay free as long as OpenRouter offers them.

What you trade for free access: rate limits. Free models allow 20 requests per minute and 200 requests per day total (without purchasing credits). That's more than enough for personal use — most people never hit that ceiling doing daily work.

### Models Worth Trying

Some of the strongest free models available right now:

- **Llama 3.3 70B** (Free) — Great for general-purpose tasks. 66K context window. Best all-rounder on the free tier.
- **Google Gemma 3 27B** (Free) — Solid for writing and analysis. 131K context window.
- **Qwen3.6** (Free) — Handles vision and tools. 1M context window — that means it can process massive documents in one go.
- **OpenRouter Free Router** — A special model ID (`openrouter/free`) that automatically picks the best available free model for you. You don't need to choose.

### Free vs "Free Trial" — The Difference

| Free Model on OpenRouter | Free Trial from AI Companies |
|---|---|
| No credit card needed | Requires card for "free" access |
| No expiration date | Expires in days or weeks |
| Rate-limited but unlimited time | Time-limited access window |
| 200 requests/day | Maybe 50 requests total |

### The Fine Print

Free model IDs end with `:free`. That's how you spot them. These models are subsidized by OpenRouter — meaning OpenRouter covers the cost. Free models can be removed or have their limits adjusted without notice. They are great for learning and daily tasks, not for running a production business at scale.

## Get Your Key in 5 Minutes

Setting up OpenRouter takes about as long as making a cup of coffee. Here's the exact path.

### Step 1: Create Your Account

Go to openrouter.ai and sign up. You'll need an email. That's it — no credit card, no phone number, no payment details. Just an email to get started with the free tier.

### Step 2: Get Your API Key

Once logged in, open the Settings page and select Keys. Click "Create Key" and give it a name — something like "My Personal Key" works fine. Copy the key immediately. You won't see it again after you close that screen.

### Step 3: Store It Somewhere Safe

Treat this key like a password. Anyone with your key can use your OpenRouter account. Do not share it, do not paste it in public forums, and do not save it on a sticky note on your monitor. A password manager or your computer's secure notes app works well.

### Step 4: Start Using It

OpenRouter works with the same format as OpenAI's API. That means any tool or app that supports OpenAI can work with OpenRouter — you just change the API endpoint URL. Or, use OpenRouter's built-in chat room to try models directly in your browser, no setup needed.

### Step 5: Set a Usage Alert (Optional but Smart)

Even though you're using free models today, you might add credits later for more powerful options. Set up a low-balance alert in Settings so you always know when you're running low. No surprise charges.
