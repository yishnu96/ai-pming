**Perplexity AI** flips that tradeoff. It is an AI-powered search engine, not a chatbot. Every question triggers a live web search, and every answer comes with numbered citations. Click any citation to verify the source yourself.

ChatGPT is a smart colleague who speaks from memory. Perplexity is a research assistant who hands you a summary with a bibliography. Both are useful. Only one lets you verify every claim.

Here is what Perplexity does not do. It does not hold long conversations. It is not built for creative writing. **You ask. It searches. It answers with sources. Done.**

:::info Think About It
When was the last time you shared an AI answer at work that someone asked you to back up? How would it feel if every claim came with a clickable source?

_Imagine never needing to hedge your AI answers with "I think..." because each fact links back to a real source._
:::

## How It Works

Google gives you ten links. Perplexity reads them for you.

You type a question. Perplexity searches the live web right now -- not a frozen training snapshot. It pulls the strongest results, reads them, and writes a concise summary. **Every claim gets a numbered reference** linking directly to the source. You do not have to trust the AI. You can click and verify.

Most AI tools freeze their knowledge on a past date. Perplexity always searches the current web because it is a search engine first.

:::info Think About It
How much time did you spend finding sources for your last report? What if an AI read the same pages and drafted your first pass in thirty seconds?
:::

## Free vs. Pro

You probably do not need to pay. Yet.

**The free tier** limits daily searches and uses standard AI models. It handles most everyday research perfectly. Think of it like a library card.

**Pro costs $20/month.** It removes the search limit, unlocks Claude and GPT-4, offers custom profiles tuned to your field, and includes API access. That is the difference between a library card and a dedicated research librarian who knows your project.

:::tip Key Takeaway
Free is enough for daily research. Go Pro only if you routinely hit search limits or need access to premium models like Claude and GPT-4.
:::

1. Open **perplexity.ai**

3. Read the answer, then click two or three citations
4. Verify each source actually supports its claim
