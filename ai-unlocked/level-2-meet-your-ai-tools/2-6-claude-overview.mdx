You know that feeling when you ask an AI a question and it fires back a confident answer that's completely wrong? You stare at the screen thinking, "That sounds right... but it isn't."

Claude was built to fix exactly that problem.

By the end of this article, you'll know what Claude is, how it differs from ChatGPT, and whether it's worth your time.

## What Is Claude

Claude is an AI assistant made by Anthropic, a company founded by researchers who previously worked at OpenAI — the same team behind ChatGPT. They left to build something different: an AI that prioritizes safety and thoughtful responses over speed.

Think of it this way. ChatGPT is the enthusiastic intern who raises their hand first in every meeting. Claude is the quiet colleague who takes a moment to think before speaking — and usually gets it right.

Anthropic's approach shows. If you asked Claude to review a 40-page contract, it wouldn't rush through it. It would carefully analyze the details and flag risks other tools might miss.

**What Claude is NOT:** Just another chatbot. It's built by the same people who helped create ChatGPT, but with one critical difference — safety over speed. Every response is designed to be helpful, honest, and harmless.

## Claude vs ChatGPT

Same DNA. Opposite priorities.

Both tools come from shared roots — the same former OpenAI researchers. But they're built for different strengths.

Claude handles long documents better, produces fewer hallucinations (made-up facts), and excels at analytical writing and summaries. It's the one you want for reviewing reports, drafting policies, or breaking down complex documents.

ChatGPT has a broader feature set — image generation, voice mode, broader integrations — and can feel faster for quick, everyday questions. It's the one you want for creative brainstorming or when you need a quick answer.

Neither is universally better. They're different tools for different jobs.

## Pick Your Plan

You don't need to pay for Claude. Not yet.

Anthropic offers a free tier that gives you everyday access to Claude's base model — perfect for learning what Claude can do. When you outgrow those limits, or need more power, paid plans unlock additional usage and features.

Here's the simple breakdown:

- **Free** — Basic access to Claude's model with daily usage limits. Good enough to get started.
- **Pro ($20/month)** — More usage than free, priority access during busy times, and access to advanced models.
- **Team ($25-30/user/month)** — Shared usage pools, admin controls, and team collaboration features.
- **Enterprise** — Unlimited usage, privacy guarantees (your data is never used for training), and SSO integration for large organizations.

The key difference between free and paid? **Usage caps, not features.** The key difference between Team and Enterprise? **Who sees your data.**

## Claude's Superpowers

Claude has one capability that sets it apart from most AI tools: a **200K context window** — meaning it can process the equivalent of a 300-page novel in a single conversation.

Context windows are how much information an AI can "hold" at once. Think of it like your working memory while reading. Most tools can only hold a few chapters. Claude can hold the entire book.

This matters when you're working with long documents — contracts, research papers, customer feedback compilations, or detailed project specs.

One important clarification: a big context window is **not the same thing as memory**. Context is what Claude can see in your current conversation. Memory is what it remembers between conversations. Claude's strength here is depth — not duration.

Beyond context, Claude consistently outperforms competitors in analytical reasoning and writing quality. The output tends to read more naturally — less robotic, more human.

## Try This Now

Here's a hands-on test you can do in under five minutes.

1. Open Claude (free) at claude.ai.
2. Upload any PDF — a report, contract, or article you're already working with.
3. Ask: **"Summarize the key points and flag anything I should pay attention to."**
4. Read the response. Then paste the same document into ChatGPT and ask the same question.
5. Compare the two. Look for: Does one catch nuances the other misses? Does one include details the other skips?

That difference is what Claude brings to the table.

:::tip Key Takeaway

Claude is the thoughtful analyst — slower to answer, better at depth. Its 200K context window processes 300-page documents in one go. The free tier handles everyday tasks. Pro unlocks advanced models. And every response prioritizes safety and accuracy over speed.
