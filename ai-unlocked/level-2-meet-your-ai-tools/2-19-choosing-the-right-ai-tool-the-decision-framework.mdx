Here's the fastest way to pick:

- **Need cited research with sources?** Perplexity. It's built like a search engine, not a chatbot. Every answer comes with links you can verify.
- **Already living in Google Workspace?** Gemini. It sits inside Docs, Gmail, and Sheets. No switching tabs.
- **Already living in Microsoft 365?** Copilot. Same logic -- it's already in Word, Excel, and Outlook.
- **Want the best all-rounder?** ChatGPT. It writes, analyzes, creates images, and holds conversations. One tool for most things.
- **Working with long documents or need fewer hallucinations?** Claude. It handles 50-page contracts, research papers, and technical docs better than most.

**Don't overthink it.** Pick the one that fits where you already work and start using it today. You can always add more later.

## Task-to-Tool Mapping

Here's a cheat sheet. Screenshot it. Put it on your phone.

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

| Task | Best Tool |
| :--- | :--- |
| Writing emails | ChatGPT or Claude |
| Deep research with sources | Perplexity |
| Analyzing a 50-page document | Claude |
| Working in Excel | Copilot |
| Working in Gmail | Gemini |
| Coding help | Claude or ChatGPT |
| Creating images | ChatGPT (DALL-E) |
| Quick answer to a question | Any tool's free tier |

</div>

Notice the pattern? **No single tool wins everything.** Each one has a strength. Your job is matching the task to the tool.

Think of it like a kitchen. You wouldn't use a blender to chop onions. You'd use a knife. Same idea here -- match the tool to the job.

## Cost at a Glance

Here's the part nobody tells you: **you don't need to pay for all of them.**

Every major AI tool -- ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot -- offers free access to basic features. You can try all of them today without spending a rupee.

The paid tiers all cost around $20 per month. And here's the truth: **most people only need ONE paid tool.**

The best AI stack costs $20/month, not $200. The other free tools round out your toolkit for tasks where your main tool falls short.
