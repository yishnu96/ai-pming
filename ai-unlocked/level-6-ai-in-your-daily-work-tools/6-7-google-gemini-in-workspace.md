You know that feeling when you stare at a blank Google Doc and nothing comes? Or when someone drops a spreadsheet with 2,000 rows and you have no idea where to start? You open the file. You close the file. You open it again.

Here is the good news: if your company uses Google Workspace, AI is already there. Not in a separate window. Not in another tab you forgot about. Gemini lives inside Docs, Sheets, Gmail, Slides, and Drive. It is built into the same apps you open every morning.

By the end of this article, you will know exactly what Gemini does in each Workspace app, how it compares to Microsoft Copilot, and three quick wins you can try right now.

## Gemini in Each App

### Docs — Writing Gets a Co-Pilot

Gemini in Docs turns your blank page anxiety into first drafts in seconds. You can ask Gemini to:

- **Write a Smart Draft** — Give it a prompt like "Write a project proposal for a Q2 marketing campaign targeting small businesses" and it generates structured text you can edit. Think of it like a colleague who hands you a rough draft instead of an empty page.
- **Create Research Summaries** — Paste a long article or report and ask Gemini to summarize the key points. What used to take fifteen minutes of skimming now takes thirty seconds.
- **Help with Citations** — Gemini can suggest relevant citations and sources for your writing, helping you back up claims without hours of research.

**What Gemini is NOT in Docs:** It is not a "publish and forget" button. The drafts it creates still need your eye, your facts, your voice. It is a starting point, not a finished product.

### Sheets — AI That Speaks Data

If spreadsheets intimidate you, Gemini in Sheets is the feature that will change your mind.

- **AI-Driven Insights** — Highlight a range of cells and ask Gemini "What trends do you see?" It spots patterns, creates charts, and explains what the data is telling you in plain English.
- **Data Cleaning** — Gemini can find duplicates, fix formatting inconsistencies, and flag missing values. It is like having a detail-obsessed assistant who never gets tired.
- **Scenario Modelling** — Ask "What happens if we increase prices by 10%?" and Gemini helps you model outcomes. You get to test ideas without writing a single formula.

**What Gemini is NOT in Sheets:** It is not a full replacement for complex financial modelling or advanced statistical analysis. For heavy lifting, you still need purpose-built tools — or a data analyst.

### Gmail — Email Writing, Faster

You have probably already seen this one. Gemini adds a "Help me write" button directly in the compose window. You type a short instruction like "Thank the client for the meeting and attach the pricing deck" and Gemini writes a professional draft. You edit. You send. Done.

### Slides — From Idea to Deck

Gemini in Slides helps you:

- **Draft Slides** — Give it a topic and it generates a slide deck outline with titles, bullet points, and speaker notes.
- **Suggest Designs** — Gemini can recommend layouts, color schemes, and image placements that match your content.

### Drive — Find and Understand Files

Gemini in Drive acts like a smart search engine for your own files. Ask it to summarize a document without opening it. Search for files using natural language ("Find the budget spreadsheet from last March") instead of exact file names.

**What Gemini is NOT across all apps:** It is not one-size-fits-all. Each app gets different capabilities. Docs gets writing help. Sheets gets data analysis. Slides gets presentation support. The AI adapts to the tool, not the other way around.

## Gemini vs. Copilot

They are not the same AI wearing different logos. Here is how they actually compare:

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

| Feature | Google Gemini (Workspace) | Microsoft Copilot (Microsoft 365) |
|:--------|:--------------------------|:----------------------------------|
| Where it lives | Built into Google Workspace apps | Built into Microsoft 365 apps |
| Email help | "Help me write" in Gmail | Compose Assist in Outlook |
| Document help | Smart Draft in Docs | Draft with Copilot in Word |
| Spreadsheet help | Insights + data cleaning in Sheets | Analyze Data in Excel |
| Presentation help | Slide drafting in Slides | Create slides from prompts in PowerPoint |
| Meeting summaries | Google Meet integration | Teams meeting recaps |
| File search | Natural language in Drive | Microsoft Graph search |
| Pricing | Included in some Workspace plans, add-on for others | Requires Copilot license ($30/user/month on top of M365) |

</div>

The biggest difference? Ecosystem lock-in. If your company runs on Google Workspace, Gemini is your native AI. If everything is in Microsoft 365, Copilot is. Neither is a drop-in replacement for the other.

## Quick Wins to Try

Here are five things you can do in under five minutes:

1. **Instant Drafts in Docs** — Open any document. Click the Gemini icon in the sidebar. Type a prompt for something you need to write. See the draft appear. Edit it. Save the time you would have spent staring at a blank page.

2. **Data Summaries in Sheets** — Highlight any table of data. Ask Gemini to explain what it shows. You will get plain-English insights instead of numbers you need to decode.

3. **Email Assistance in Gmail** — Next time you compose an email, click "Help me write." Give Gemini a one-sentence instruction. Watch it draft three versions you can choose from.

4. **Slides Storyboarding** — Open Slides. Ask Gemini to create an outline for a presentation topic. You get structure in seconds instead of an hour spent on slide order.

5. **Drive File Lookup** — Instead of scrolling through folders, type what you need in natural language. "Find the Q3 report" works just as well as remembering the exact filename.

Each of these is a quick win — a single action that saves you time today. They are not a full workflow. They are your first step. Once you see Gemini work, you will start noticing where else it fits.

:::info Think About It
Which quick win will you try first? Pick one. Do it today. The best time to start using AI is when the task is still fresh.
:::
