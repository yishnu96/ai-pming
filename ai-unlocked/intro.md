# AI Unlocked

Welcome to **AI Unlocked** — your structured learning path covering AI fundamentals through practical applications.

Whether you're a Product Manager, Marketing Leader, Sales Head, or CXO, this guide will help you understand and leverage AI in your day-to-day work.

## What You Will Learn

1. **Introduction** — LLMs, transformers, embeddings, and AI agents
2. **AI Foundation** — RAG, fine-tuning, vector databases, and more
3. **Gen AI** — Capabilities, limitations, and the model landscape
4. **Prompting** — Techniques and frameworks for effective prompting
5. **Increasing AI Capabilities** — MCP, skills, multi-agents, and tools
6. **Being Superhuman** — CLI mastery and Git in the AI era
7. **All About Claude Code** — Deep dive into Claude Code
8. **Evaluations** — Measuring and improving AI output quality
9. **Building AI Agents** — APIs, automation platforms, and agent development
10. **AI for Leaders** — Practical applications for PMs, PMMs, and CXOs
11. **Caution!** — Validation, security, and cost management
